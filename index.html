Key Features

Advanced Security

Our system employs state-of-the-art encryption and access control to ensure your data is safe from unauthorized access. Regular security updates keep the system resilient to emerging threats.

Scalable Architecture

Built on a scalable infrastructure, our system can handle increasing loads as your lab or institution grows. Seamlessly add more users and manage a growing inventory without performance degradation.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed with simplicity in mind, our platform is intuitive and easy to navigate. Whether you're a lab manager or professor, you can accomplish tasks efficiently without extensive training.

About the Project

This project was created to address the difficulties lab managers and professors face in managing chemical inventories. It's designed to streamline the process of ordering and tracking chemical products, making lab operations more efficient.

It is also part of my portfolio project for Holberton School. The timeline spanned several months, from ideation to design, development, and deployment.
